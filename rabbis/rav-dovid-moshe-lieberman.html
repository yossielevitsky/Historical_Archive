Rav Dovid Moshe Lieberman

הרב דוד משה ליברמן

Introduction: Rav Dovid Moshe Lieberman (1925–2022) served as the Chief Rabbi of the Shomre Hadas community in Antwerp for over four decades. He was a revered spiritual leader who guided the community through its significant post-war expansion.

Life & Career

Born in Germany in 1925, Rav Lieberman survived the Holocaust and went on to study in yeshivot in the United States. He was appointed as the Chief Rabbi of Shomre Hadas in 1980, a position he held with great distinction until his passing.

Legacy

Rav Lieberman is credited with unifying the diverse elements of the Antwerp Jewish community. His wisdom, humility, and accessibility made him a beloved figure across all sectors of the community. He was instrumental in maintaining the high standards of Kashrut and education for which Antwerp is known.
