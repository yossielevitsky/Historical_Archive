Rav Pinchas Padwa

הרב פנחס פאדווא

Introduction: Rav Pinchas Padwa is a prominent rabbinical authority who served as the Chief Rabbi of the Machsike Hadass community in Antwerp. He is known for his Halachic expertise and his leadership within the Chareidi community.

Life & Career

Before his tenure in Antwerp, Rav Padwa served in various rabbinical capacities. As the spiritual leader of Machsike Hadass, he oversaw the community's religious infrastructure, including kashrut, eruv, and mikvaot.

Legacy

His tenure is marked by a strict adherence to Halacha and the strengthening of the community's religious institutions.
