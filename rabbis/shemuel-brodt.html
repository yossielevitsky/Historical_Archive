Shemu’el Brodt

שמואל ברודט

Introduction: Shemu’el Brodt (1885–1963) was a significant figure in the Antwerp Jewish community. Known for his dedicated service and rabbinical knowledge, he played a key role in the religious life of the city.

Life & Career

Detailed biographical information is currently being researched. He served the community with distinction during his lifetime, spanning the pre-war and post-war eras.

Legacy

Shemu’el Brodt is remembered for his contributions to the rebuilding of Jewish life in Antwerp after World War II.
