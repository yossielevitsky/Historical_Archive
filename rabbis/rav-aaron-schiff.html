Rav Aaron Schiff

הרב אהרן שיף

Introduction: Rav Aaron Schiff is the current Chief Rabbi of the Machsike Hadass community in Antwerp (as of the early 21st century). He is a respected scholar and leader who has continued the traditions of his predecessors.

Life & Career

Rav Schiff was appointed to lead the Machsike Hadass community, one of the most vibrant Orthodox communities in Europe. His leadership is characterized by a focus on education and the maintenance of traditional values.

Legacy

Under his guidance, the community has continued to flourish, maintaining its status as a major center of Jewish life in Europe.
