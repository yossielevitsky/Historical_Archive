Rav Hillel Medalie

הרב הלל מדליה

Introduction: Rav Hillel Medalie (1916–1977) was a distinguished Rabbi who served the Shomre Hadas community in Antwerp. He was known for his charisma, his eloquence, and his deep connection to his congregants.

Life & Career

Born in 1916, Rav Medalie came from a prominent rabbinic family. Before coming to Antwerp, he held positions in Leeds and Dublin. In Antwerp, he became a central figure in the post-war reconstruction of the community.

Legacy

Rav Medalie is remembered for his powerful speeches and his ability to connect with the younger generation. He played a crucial role in stabilizing the community in the decades following the war.
