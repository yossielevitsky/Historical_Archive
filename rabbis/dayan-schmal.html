Dayan Schmal

דיין שמאל

Introduction: Dayan Schmal was a highly respected rabbinical judge (Dayan) in Antwerp. His knowledge of Jewish law and his fair judgments earned him the respect of the entire community.

Life & Career

Serving on the Beit Din (Rabbinical Court), Dayan Schmal dealt with complex questions of Jewish law and arbitraged disputes within the community. His dedication to truth and peace was exemplary.

Legacy

He is remembered as a model of integrity and scholarship, contributing significantly to the smooth functioning of the community's religious life.
