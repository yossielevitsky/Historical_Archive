Kehilles of Antwerp

The three main Jewish communities (Kehilles) that form the pillar of religious life.

Israëlitische Gemeente van Antwerpen Shomre Hadass

Main Community • Est. 19th Century

The oldest and largest Jewish community in Antwerp, often referred to as the "Dutch Community". It oversees several central synagogues.

Orthodox Israëlitische Gemeente Machsike Hadass

Ultra-Orthodox • Est. 1892

Founded to maintain strict Orthodox standards, Machsike Hadass serves a large Hasidic population and manages its own Kashrut and religious institutions.

Portuguees Israëlitische Gemeenschap van Antwerpen

Sephardic • Historical

Representing the historical Sephardic community, preserving distinct liturgical traditions and a rich cultural heritage dating back centuries.
