What We Are Looking For

We are actively seeking contributions to enrich the Jewish Antwerp Historical Archive. Specifically, we are looking for:

  • Historical photographs of synagogues, community events, and daily life.
  • Documents, letters, and artifacts related to Jewish life in Antwerp.
  • Personal stories and testimonies from community members.
  • Videos or audio recordings of historical significance.
  • Corrections or additional information for existing profiles and articles.
