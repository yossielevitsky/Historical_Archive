Beit Yaakov

בית הכנסת בית יעקב

The Beit Yaakov synagogue is a significant place of worship located on Jacob Jacobstraat. It has served the community for decades.

History

Detailed historical information for this synagogue is currently being researched. It has played a vital role in the religious life of the neighborhood.
