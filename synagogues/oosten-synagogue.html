Oosten Synagogue

בית הכנסת אוסטן

The Oosten Synagogue is a majestic building reflecting the grandeur of Antwerp's Jewish community in the early 20th century.

Architecture

Known for its striking Art Nouveau features mixed with other architectural styles, it stands as a prominent landmark in the Jewish quarter.
