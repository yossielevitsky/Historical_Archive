Pshevorsk Synagogue

בית המדרש דחסידי פשעווארסק

The Pshevorsk Synagogue serves as the center for the Pshevorsk Hasidic dynasty in Antwerp. It is a hub of prayer, study, and community gatherings.

Community

Pshevorsk is one of the most prominent Hasidic courts in Antwerp, known for its warm hospitality and charitable activities.
