Eisenmann Synagoge

בית הכנסת אייזנמן

Founded by Jacques Eisenmann, this synagogue is a unique historical gem that survived WWII relatively intact. It is known for its intimate atmosphere and beautiful interior.

History

Inaugurated in 1907, the Eisenmann Synagogue was established by Jacques Eisenmann for the growing Jewish community in Antwerp. Unlike many other synagogues, its interior was not destroyed during the German occupation, preserving its original character.
